Important: Smart Leaf provides informational plant disease guidance only. It is not a substitute for professional agricultural or plant pathology advice.

Smart Leaf is a mobile app that helps you identify plant leaf diseases using artificial intelligence running entirely on your device. No account required, no cloud uploads — just point, scan, and learn.

What it does

Smart Leaf analyzes photos of plant leaves to predict possible diseases. Use your phone's camera for a live scan, or pick an image from your gallery. The app returns a diagnosis with a confidence score and links to a detailed disease guide covering symptoms, treatment, and prevention.

A built-in library lets you browse common diseases across crops like tomato, corn, apple, grape, and more — even when you're offline.

How it works

Smart Leaf ships with a TensorFlow Lite model (smartleaf_fp16) bundled inside the app. When you scan a leaf, the image is preprocessed and run through the model on your phone's processor. Results appear in seconds without any network connection.

You can adjust the confidence threshold in Settings to control how strict the app is before reporting a match.

Privacy first

Your scan photos and history are stored locally on your device. We don't operate a backend server and don't collect personal data. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
